Since the start of 2024, I have been organizing programs and events for undergraduates at UW-Madison who are interested in category theory and adjacent topics.

During the spring of 2024, I ran a reading group and organized two talks. The reading group covered most of Tom Leinster's Basic Category Theory. The first talk was given by Jack Westbrook, a UW-Madison undergraduate, on abelian categories. The second talk was given by me on point-free topology.

Future potential talk subjects include combinatorial species and sheaves.

If you would like more information about these programs and events, feel free to contact me using the information here.
